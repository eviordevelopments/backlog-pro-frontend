# Product Overview

**Backlog Pro - Agile Suite** is a comprehensive Agile + DevOps Product Backlog Management System built by E-vior Developments.

## Core Purpose

Unifies the entire SDLC, SCRUM, KPIs, DevOps metrics, and risk management in a single platform. Eliminates work fragmentation across multiple tools (Notion, Jira, Excel) by consolidating Agile management, DevOps metrics, risks, and finances in one place.

## Target Users

- Development teams
- Founders and product managers
- Scrum Masters
- Startups seeking speed, clarity, and control

## Key Features

1. **Task Management** - Full CRUD with story points, priorities, and assignees
2. **User Stories** - INVEST format with acceptance criteria
3. **Kanban Board** - Drag & drop with real-time updates
4. **Sprint Management** - Planning, tracking, burn-down charts, velocity
5. **KPI Dashboard** - Team and individual metrics (velocity, cycle time, completion rate)
6. **Team Profiles** - Editable profiles with individual KPIs for Pedro, David, Morena, Franco
7. **Risk Matrix** - 5x5 interactive matrix with automatic scoring
8. **Profit Sharing** - Revenue distribution calculations
9. **DevOps Dashboard** - DORA metrics (deployment frequency, lead time, MTTR, change failure rate)

## Design Philosophy

Modern glassmorphic design with:
- Deep indigo primary (#5B63F5)
- Teal accent (#14B8A6)
- Semantic color tokens for success, warning, destructive states
- Smooth transitions and hover effects
- Mobile-first responsive approach
